Hello World Again :-)

This is ZYT’s blog. I say “Hello World Again” because I setup this Hexo blog in spring break earlier, but stopped due to school work and my laziness. Last week, after the summer session, I finally finished a tiny project started in the spring break. What coming next is this blog.

I will start with sharing some photographs taken last summer vacation. Then, I will share my notes on Scoop, Vim, gcov, and other technical topics. A journey of a thousand miles begins with single step.
